Why Choose Us

Transform Your Look, Transform Your Confidence

Renowned makeup artist Farha Siddiqui prioritizes quality above all when it comes to selecting products for her clients. She utilizes internationally renowned and thoroughly tested brands, ranging from luxury labels like Dior to heritage favorites such as M.A.C cosmetics. Always on top of what’s trending in the beauty market, she continually updates her techniques and materials, ensuring her clients always get the best.

She was acknowledged by Bollywood legend Kareena Kapoor Khan in 2019, who presented her with an award for ‘The Most Trusted Bridal Studio and Academy’. In 2021, she further bolstered her reputation by winning an accolade at the Wedding Sutra Influencer Awards. Choosing Farha Siddiqui means investing in an award-winning artist who is dedicated to bringing out your natural beauty, utilizing top-quality products and the latest trends.
